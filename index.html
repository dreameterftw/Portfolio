§ About

A short note.

I'm pursuing B.Tech Computer Science Engineering with a specialisation in Data Science — about to start my second year. Most of what I've actually learned has come from shipping things: seeing what breaks, what people use, what feels right at 11pm on a Tuesday.

My work tends to start with a frustration — a learner who doesn't know what to study next, a person trying to read a contract they didn't write, someone with a symptom and twelve open tabs. The interfaces I build try to take the noise out of that, and make the next step obvious without being loud about it.

Outside of code: I follow football religiously (Chelsea till I die), spend time at the gym, watch anime, and read manga. Occasionally all four in the same day.
